About the Artist

With a pencil in hand since childhood, I've dedicated my life to capturing the essence of humanity through the simple yet profound medium of graphite and charcoal. Each stroke tells a story, each shadow reveals an emotion.

Graduating from the Academy of Fine Arts with honors, I've since exhibited in galleries across Europe and North America, with works held in private collections worldwide.
